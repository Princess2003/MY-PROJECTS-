Welcome to Reliable House Centre

Empowering Lives through Education, Shelter, Entrepreneurship, and Employment

Visit Us

Empowering Through Education

Our programs are designed to cater to the needs of those who stay in the streets of Pretoria and use drugs, providing them with a supportive and understanding environment where they can learn and grow.

Basic Literacy and Numeracy

Our basic literacy and numeracy program helps individuals improve their reading, writing, and math skills, laying a strong foundation for further education and employment opportunities.

Vocational Training

We offer vocational training in various trades, providing practical skills that can lead to immediate employment. Our training programs include carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and more.

Life Skills Workshops

Our life skills workshops cover essential topics such as financial literacy, communication skills, and personal development, helping individuals build confidence and independence.

Entrepreneurship Classes

For those interested in starting their own business, our entrepreneurship classes offer guidance on business planning, marketing, and financial management, empowering individuals to create their own opportunities.
